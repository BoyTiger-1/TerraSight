02 / How it works

From raw satellite feed to a decision you can defend.

Gather

Live and historical data pulled from authoritative sources: weather models, reanalysis, river gauges, seismometers, fire detections, storm advisories. No manual entry, no synthetic filler.

Model

Gradient-boosted models trained on real documented disasters, physics-constrained so more wind can never mean less fire. Where prediction is impossible, honest impact models take over.

Explain

Every score ships with the variables that drove it, a confidence label, and the model card behind it. No black boxes, because nobody evacuates a town on a mystery number.

Act

Role-targeted recommendations for residents, emergency managers, utilities, farmers, and insurers, plus economic exposure so the cost of waiting is visible.

03 / Coupled systems

Disasters do not happen one at a time.

Drought primes wildfire. Hurricanes drive flood, surge, and blackout at once. Earthquakes trigger tsunamis and landslides. TerraSight models twenty documented couplings between hazards, so when one score rises, everything downstream updates with it.

04 / Honest science

Prediction where possible. Impact modeling where not.

No one on Earth can predict an earthquake, and TerraSight will not pretend to. For earthquakes, tsunamis, and volcanoes the platform models what science actually supports: exposure, shaking, aftershock probabilities, evacuation priorities, and losses, built on fifty years of real catalog data.
